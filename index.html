About Tshami Holdings

Established as a forward-thinking Human Resources (HR) company, Tshami Holdings serves as the parent entity of recruitment, training, and farming subsidiaries. The group is committed to alleviating unemployment, skill development, and learning across its subsidiaries.

Our Mission

To connect great companies with great employees, drive transformation through skills development, create long-term stakeholder value, and uphold ethical, sustainable business practices

Our Approach

We combine strategic expertise with hands-on implementation, ensuring sustainable impact across staffing, training, and agricultural sectors.

Our Subsidiaries

Tshami Holdings operates through a decentralized model, allowing each subsidiary a degree of independence while ensuring alignment with the group's strategic objectives. The leadership team at the holding company level provides oversight and strategic guidance while empowering subsidiary management to make operational decisions.

Tshami Staffing Solutions

Tshami Staffing Solutions was established as an intelligent and sound recruitment and placement agency positioned to provide permanent and contract-based staff. Its portfolio caters to various industries with the aim of providing a state-of-the-art recruitment solution for both career seekers and clients.

Kamoka Banareng Training

Accredited with AgriSeta, Kamoka Banareng Training operates as a training entity focusing on agricultural-related courses. The training is designed to develop and empower communities with the knowledge and appropriate skills. The training offers holistic courses that provide skills to enable individuals to adapt when facing numerous challenges such as climate change, food demand, population growth, and depleting natural resources.

Vanwanati Farms

Vanwanati Farms is an emerging experiential farm. This subsidiary focuses on providing hands-on farming experiences and education. More information is being loaded about this exciting venture.

Group Structure

Tshami Holdings uses a decentralized structure where subsidiaries operate independently, guided by the holding company's strategic oversight and leadership.

Corporate Governance and Leadership

The governance structure at Tshami Holdings is designed to ensure accountability, transparency, and ethical conduct. The board of directors includes experienced leaders with diverse backgrounds. Together, they provide strategic direction and oversee risk management. Each subsidiary is managed by its own executive team, responsible for day-to-day operations and performance.

Commitment to Sustainability and Social Responsibility

Tshami Holdings places a strong emphasis on corporate social responsibility (CSR). The group supports initiatives in education, community development, farming, and economic empowerment. Through these efforts, Tshami Holdings strives to make a positive impact on society and contribute to the sustainable development of the communities in which it operates

Tshami Holdings exemplifies a commitment to growth, sustainability, and social responsibility. With its diverse subsidiaries, the company aims to create job opportunities, enhance skills, and promote ethical business practices. By focusing on both economic and social value, Tshami Holdings is poised to make a lasting impact on the industries it serves and the communities it touches.
